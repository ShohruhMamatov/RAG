Academic Programs and Research at New Uzbekistan University

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS:

New Uzbekistan University offers diverse undergraduate programs across four schools:

School of Engineering:
1. Chemical and Materials Engineering
   - Focus on materials science and chemical processes
   - Special scholarship available starting 2024-2025
   - Industry partnerships for practical experience

2. Mechanical Engineering
   - Modern mechanical systems and design
   - Special scholarship available starting 2024-2025
   - Hands-on laboratory work

School of Computing and Technology:
3. Software Engineering
   - Software development and engineering principles
   - Cutting-edge programming and technologies
   - Industry-relevant curriculum

4. Cybersecurity
   - Information security and digital protection
   - Network security and ethical hacking
   - High-demand field

5. Artificial Intelligence and Robotics
   - AI algorithms and machine learning
   - Robotics design and implementation
   - Innovation-focused program

School of Sciences:
6. Applied Mathematics
   - Mathematical modeling and analysis
   - Computational mathematics
   - Research opportunities

School of Social Sciences and Management:
7. Economics and Data Science
   - Economic theory and data analytics
   - Big data and business intelligence
   - GMAT/GRE considered for graduate admissions

8. Pedagogy
   - Education theory and teaching methods
   - Curriculum development
   - Educational leadership

9. Industrial Management
   - Operations and supply chain management
   - Business strategy and leadership
   - Industry connections
