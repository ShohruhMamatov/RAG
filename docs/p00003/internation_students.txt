International Students and Campus Life at New Uzbekistan University

STUDY IN UZBEKISTAN:

New Uzbekistan University welcomes international students from around the world to experience world-class education in the heart of Central Asia.

Benefits for International Students:
- Internationally recognized degrees
- Global partnerships with MIT and TUM
- English-medium instruction
- Multicultural campus environment
- Affordable cost of living in Tashkent
- Safe and welcoming environment
- Modern facilities and accommodation

Visa and Arrival:
- International students require student visa to study in Uzbekistan
- University provides visa support and documentation
- Guidance available through International Office
- Arrival assistance for new students

Entry Requirements:
- Completed secondary education (for undergraduate)
- Bachelor's degree (for graduate programs)
- English proficiency certificates (IELTS, TOEFL iBT)
- Valid passport
- Health insurance

Talent Referral Program:
- Special program for referring talented students
- Benefits for both referrer and referred student
- Details available on university website

Campus Life & Facilities
Dormitories:

Dormitory 1: Located within the University of World Economy and Diplomacy (UWED) residence.

Dormitory 2: Located at the Abdulla Avloniy National Institute.

Cost: Approx. 640,000 UZS per month (covers utilities).

Facilities:

Main Auditorium & Technology Hub (MATH): Includes lecture halls, seminar rooms, and specialized labs (Physics, Chemistry, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering).

Library: Comprehensive resources to support academic study.

Student Services: Academic advising, mental health support, and career counseling.

Clubs & Activities: A vibrant student council, sports clubs, and cultural events.
