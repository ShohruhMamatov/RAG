Graduate Admissions at New Uzbekistan University

New Uzbekistan University offers innovative and industry-relevant graduate programs (Master's degrees) designed to equip students with advanced knowledge and skills for leadership roles in academia and industry.

Why Choose NUU Graduate Programs?

1. Internationally Recognized Education:
- Curriculum designed to meet global standards
- Aligned with top universities worldwide
- High-quality education recognized internationally

2. State-of-the-Art Research Facilities:
- Modern laboratories
- Advanced research centers
- Resources for hands-on learning and scientific discovery

3. Strong Industry Connections:
- Collaboration with leading companies
- Practical experience opportunities
- Professional networking
- Industry-led projects

4. Double-Degree Programs:
- Selected programs offer opportunity to earn two degrees
- International partnerships

5. Experienced Faculty:
- Top-tier professors
- Industry experts
- Real-world expertise in classroom

Program Types:
- One-year graduate programs
- Two-year graduate programs

Academic Programs:
Graduate programs are offered across four schools in fields including:
- Engineering
- Computing and Technology
- Applied Sciences
- Economics and Data Science
- Management

Admission Requirements:

1. Educational Background:
- Bachelor's degree diploma
- Academic transcripts from previous institution

2. English Proficiency:
- IELTS certificate
- TOEFL iBT certificate (only approved test centers)
- CEFR certificate
Note: CEFR certificates are NOT accepted for double-degree programs. Only TOEFL iBT from approved test centers accepted for double-degree programs. TOEFL iBT Home Edition is NOT accepted.

3. Additional Tests (if applicable):
- GMAT/GRE certificate (only for Economics and Data Science program, if available)

4. Entrance Exam:
- Required for applicants to two-year graduate programs
- Not required for one-year programs

Admissions Timeline:

1. Start of Admission: February 21st
2. Application Review: Within 3 working days after submission
3. Additional Documents (if incomplete): Within 2 working days after email notification
4. Registration Deadline:
   - Two-year programs: August 20th
   - One-year programs: September 5th
5. Exam Dates (two-year programs only):
   - First exam: April 26th
   - Second exam: June 14th
   - Third exam: August 23rd
   Note: Must bring permit card and passport or ID card to exam venue
6. Final Document Submission Deadline: September 5th
