Tuition Fees and Scholarships at New Uzbekistan University

New Uzbekistan University is committed to supporting talented students through various scholarship opportunities and competitive tuition fees.

UNDERGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIPS:

1. One-Year Scholarships:
- Cover tuition fees for one academic year only
- Awarded competitively to local students
- Based on top scores in entrance exam or international exams (SAT, IB, International AS & A Levels)
- Also awarded annually to second, third, and fourth-year local students based on high academic performance in all courses

2. Four-Year Scholarships:
- Cover 100% of tuition fees for all four academic years
- Full scholarship for entire undergraduate degree
- Highly competitive

3. Special Engineering Scholarships:
- Commencing in 2024-2025 academic year
- Special scholarships awarded to students in:
  * Mechanical Engineering program
  * Chemical and Materials Engineering program

GRADUATE SCHOLARSHIPS:

1. One-Year Graduate Scholarships:
- Cover tuition fees for one academic year
- Available for graduate students

2. Two-Year Graduate Scholarships:
- Cover 100% of tuition fees for all two academic years
- Full scholarship for Master's degree duration

INTERNATIONAL STUDENT SCHOLARSHIPS:

New Uzbekistan University offers merit-based scholarships of up to 40% for international applicants.

Selection Process:

Stage 1: Academic Ranking
- All international applicants ranked based on IELTS and SAT Math scores (or country-specific equivalents)
- Top-performing applicants invited to interview

Stage 2: Interview Assessment
- Evaluates academic potential
- Assesses readiness for university life
- Reviews personal motivations

Scholarship Award:
- Up to 40% tuition fee reduction
- Based on combined evaluation of academic scores and interview performance
- Only most promising candidates receive support

TUITION FEES:

Undergraduate Programs:
- Tuition fees vary by program
- Information available on scholarships page
- Competitive rates compared to international standards

Graduate Programs (2025/2026 Academic Year):
- Specific fees listed on official website
- Varies by program and duration (one-year vs two-year)
- Calculator tool available to estimate costs

Additional Costs:
Students should budget for:
- Accommodation (dormitory options available)
- Transportation
- Study materials and textbooks
- Meals and dining
- Personal expenses
- Health insurance

Payment Information:
- Tuition fee payment information available on university website
- Payment deadlines aligned with academic calendar

Note: For most current and detailed tuition fee information, visit the official scholarships and tuition fees pages.

Scholarships
Types:

4-Year Scholarships: Covers 100% of tuition for all 4 years.

1-Year Scholarships: Covers tuition for the first year only.

Need-Based Scholarship: Full tuition coverage (100%) for 4 years for students listed in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" who pass the entrance exam.

Olympiad Winners: 100% scholarship for winners of international Olympiads (IMO, IPhO, IChO, IOI, IBO).

International Students: Merit-based scholarships of up to 40% based on academic ranking and interview.

Partial Scholarships: Some programs offer tiered scholarships (eg, 70% or 50% tuition waiver) based on entrance scores.
