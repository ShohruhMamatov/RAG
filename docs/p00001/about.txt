About New Uzbekistan University (NewUU)

New Uzbekistan University (NewUU) is a premier higher education institution in Uzbekistan, established to provide world-class education and foster innovation. The university is committed to shaping the next generation of leaders and innovators across diverse fields including engineering, computing, social sciences, and management.

Vision & Mission:
New Uzbekistan University aims to embody the pinnacle of educational achievements not only for Uzbekistan but also for the entire world. It serves as a flagship institution for the country's higher education system, partnering with global institutions like MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology) and TUM (Technical University of Munich) to deliver internationally recognized programs.

Key Features:
- Dynamic and rigorous academic environment
- Cutting-edge laboratories and research facilities
- Modern campus with collaborative co-working spaces
- Comprehensive library and learning resources
- Strong focus on innovation and practical skills
- Industry partnerships with leading companies
- Vibrant campus life with student organizations, cultural events, and leadership opportunities

Academic Structure:
The university is organized into four main schools offering diverse programs across:
- Engineering (Chemical and Materials Engineering, Mechanical Engineering)
- Computing and Technology (Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence and Robotics)
- Sciences (Applied Mathematics)
- Social Sciences and Management (Economics and Data Science, Pedagogy, Industrial Management)

Global Partnerships:
NUU collaborates with prestigious international institutions and industry companies to provide students with exposure to real-world challenges. These partnerships facilitate:
- Internships and industry-led projects
- Mentorship programs
- Research funding and state-of-the-art technologies
- Double-degree program opportunities
- Exchange programs for international students
