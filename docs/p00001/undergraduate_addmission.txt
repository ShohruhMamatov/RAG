Undergraduate Admissions at New Uzbekistan University

New Uzbekistan University offers comprehensive undergraduate programs for high-achieving students driven by curiosity and passion for knowledge.

Admission Requirements:

1. Educational Background:
- Must have completed secondary school, academic lyceum, or professional college
- Must provide diploma with academic transcripts
- High school transcripts required
- Personal statement
- Recommendation letters

2. English Language Proficiency:
Applicants must demonstrate English proficiency through:
- IELTS (minimum score requirements apply)
- TOEFL iBT (only tests taken at approved test centers are accepted)
- TOEFL iBT Home Edition is NOT accepted
- CEFR certificates

3. Math & Logical Thinking Entrance Exam:
- All applicants must pass the Math & Logical Thinking entrance exam
- Exam format: 40 multiple-choice questions
- Duration: 120 minutes (2 hours)
- Questions based on Uzbekistan general secondary and secondary specialized professional education curricula
- Developed by Cambridge University Press & Assessment
- Mix of easy, medium, and difficult questions
- Exams are broadcast online using video surveillance for transparency

4. International Certificate Exemptions:
Applicants with international certificates may be exempt from the entrance exam:
- SAT (Math section scores will be considered)
- International Baccalaureate (IB)
- International AS & A Levels

Note: Applicants can use the online equivalency calculator to compare international certificate math scores with entrance exam scores. If applicants prefer, they may still take the entrance exam. The higher score between entrance exam and international certificate will be considered.

Application Timeline:
- Application Deadline: June 26th
- Exam dates are announced on the university website

Admissions
Undergraduate Admissions
Application Timeline:

Start: January 20th

Deadline: June 26th

Exam Date: July 5th

English Requirement:

IELTS: minimum 5.5

TOEFL iBT: 46 minimum (Home Edition not accepted)

CEFR: B2

Entrance Exam:

Subject: Math & Logical Thinking.

Format: 40 multiple-choice questions, 120 minutes.

Provider: Conducted in collaboration with Cambridge University Press & Assessment.

Exemptions (Exam Waiver): Applicants with the following international certificates may be exempt from the entrance exam and receive full scores:

SAT: Math score of 650 or higher.

International Baccalaureate (IB): Minimum Math score of 4.

A-Levels: Minimum Math grade of E.

Graduate Admissions
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution (min. 180 ECTS).

English Proficiency: Generally IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL iBT 60 (varies slightly by program; eg, Modern Physics accepts IELTS 5.5).

Entrance Exam: Required for two-year programs (subjects vary, eg, Math & Physics for Modern Physics). Interview assessment for some programs.
